# AI Engineering Resources

A curated list of high-quality learning resources, research papers, and tools to accelerate your path to becoming a production-grade AI Engineer.

---

## 1. Essential Books & Reading
* **Deep Learning** by Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, and Aaron Courville: The definitive textbook on neural networks and mathematics.
* **Designing Machine Learning Systems** by Chip Huyen: Real-world production architectures, data pipelines, and system trade-offs.
* **Understanding Deep Learning** by Simon J.D. Prince: Excellent visual explanations of modern architectures.

## 2. Interactive Courses & Tutorials
* **Deep Learning Specialization** (Coursera/Andrew Ng): Excellent conceptual groundwork.
* **Neural Networks: Zero to Hero** (Andrej Karpathy): The gold standard for understanding backpropagation, GPT architectures, and building from scratch.
* **Hugging Face NLP Course**: Hands-on guide to utilizing modern Transformer pipelines and datasets.

## 3. Libraries & Production Tools
* **Modeling & Science**:
  - `numpy`: High-performance vector/matrix computations.
  - `scikit-learn`: Classical ML algorithms and utilities.
  - `pytorch`: Leading deep learning research and production framework.
* **Serving & GenAI**:
  - `vllm`: Extremely high-throughput LLM serving engine.
  - `langchain` / `llamaindex`: Orchestration layers for RAG and Agents.
  - `ollama`: Running open models locally for prototyping.
